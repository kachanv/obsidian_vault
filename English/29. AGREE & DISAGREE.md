1. **Agreeing**
	- *I feel the same way.*
	- *I agree.*  
	- *You're right.*  
	- *That's true/right.*  
2. **Disagreeing**
	- *I don't agree.*  
	- *I don't think so.*  
	- *I'm not sure that you're right.*
3. **Expressing your negative opinion**
	- *I hate it when people...*
	- *I hate that.*  
	- *It's awful.*  
	- *It's so annoying.*
